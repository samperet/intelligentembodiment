# Intelligent Embodiment — Growth Playbook

The goal: help the right people find Mackensie, understand what medical
intuition is, and take one low-risk step — a **$75 initial consult with a
money-back guarantee**.

## 1. Positioning

**One line:** *Thirty years of healing hands, now with the training to name
what they find.*

- The credibility engine is the story: three decades of bodywork → clients
  kept asking "how did you know?" → formal training with Skylar Acamesis.
- We never claim diagnosis or cure. Medical intuition **complements** medical
  care ("better questions to bring your doctor"). This is both ethical and
  what keeps ads approved on Google/Meta.
- The risk reversal (money-back guarantee) is the answer to skepticism — lead
  with it everywhere.

## 2. The funnel (already built)

| Stage | Asset |
|---|---|
| Discover | Ads/posts → `/medical-intuition` (tagged links from the admin link builder) |
| Learn | Landing page: what it is, how it works, FAQ, disclaimer |
| Trust | Testimonials (carousel + `/share` collection flow), her story on `/about` |
| Act | Risk-free Initial Consult booking, embedded on the landing page |
| Measure | UTM tags flow into each booking's notification/email ("via instagram / paid / …") |
| Retain | Newsletter list (admin: Copy emails), retreats page, writings/recipes |

## 3. Channels, in priority order

1. **Warm circle (weeks 1–2, $0).** Newsletter announcement (draft in the
   admin Marketing Studio) to the existing list; personal notes to past
   clients; ask 5 loyal clients to share a testimonial via `/share`.
2. **Local SEO ($0).** Create/claim a Google Business Profile ("Medical
   Intuitive / Massage — Charlotte, VT"), link the landing page, ask happy
   clients for Google reviews. Vermont searches are low-competition.
3. **Instagram organic (ongoing).** 2–3 posts/week alternating: client-story
   ("the shoulder that held grief"), education ("what medical intuition is
   and isn't"), and practice glimpses (qigong, kitchari, retreat photos).
   Copy starters live in the Marketing Studio.
4. **Google Search ads (from week 3, ~$300–450/mo).** Campaign on intent
   terms: "medical intuitive", "medical intuitive near me", "intuitive
   healer Vermont", "chronic pain alternative Vermont". Send to
   `/medical-intuition` with a tagged link. Start $10–15/day, kill anything
   without a booking after ~$100 spend on that ad group.
5. **Meta/IG ads (after organic proof, ~$200–300/mo).** Boost the best
   organic post to women 35–65 within ~50 mi of Burlington + interest in
   yoga/ayurveda/energy healing.
6. **Podcasts & partnerships (ongoing).** Vermont wellness podcasts, yoga
   studios, women's circles — offer a free talk: "What your body knows that
   your labs don't."

## 4. Measurement

- Every external link gets UTM tags via the admin **Campaign link builder**.
- Each booking arrives with its source in the push notification, owner email,
  and calendar event description ("Source: instagram / paid / …").
- Weekly 10-minute review: bookings by source → double down on what books,
  cut what doesn't. Target: cost per consult under $50; a consult that
  converts to 3+ massage sessions or intuition packages pays for the month.

## 5. 90-day plan

- **Days 1–14:** newsletter announcement; 5 testimonial asks; Google Business
  Profile live; 6 organic IG posts queued.
- **Days 15–45:** Google Search campaign live ($10/day); publish 2 writings
  on intuition (the admin editor); first podcast pitch batch.
- **Days 46–90:** boost best IG post; review UTM data; raise budget only on
  channels that produced consults; collect 10+ Google reviews.

## 6. Compliance guardrails

- Never promise diagnosis, treatment, or cure; always "complements medical
  care." The landing page carries a standing disclaimer.
- Money-back guarantee is honored unconditionally — it's the brand.
- Testimonials must be real and volunteered (the `/share` flow with the
  admin approval queue guarantees this).
